MY LEARNING PLAN - DRAFT

4 April 2019

My strengths:

Dedication, seeing a big picture, motivation to change my career, high orientation on logic, good interaction with people.

My limitations:

Not always following instructions and looking for different paths instead, hard to get into flow if I’m not absolutely passionate about a topic, problems with focusing on end-delivery if project is too long

I plan my days using icalendar. I block certain times to complete my work and try to stick to the plan. If I find it hard to focus, I take a few minutes break to relax. I’m being profesional and always deliver what I have promised.

I try my best to find solutions to problems I encounter by myself. I will reach out to my fellow colleagues on slack if I get stuck for longer than 30-45 minutes. I will reach out to EDA staff if other students are not able to help me.
